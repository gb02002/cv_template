\cvsection{Projects}
\small{
\subsection*{Operation: Nostromo Self-Destruct and Evacuation}
\textit{Critical incident response, June 2122} \\
In a high-stress scenario with limited resources, devised and executed a plan to destroy the ship while ensuring the escape of the sole survivor (Jonesy the cat). Successfully piloted the shuttle "Narcissus" to safety. \\
\textit{Key outcomes:} Neutralized the xenomorph threat, prevented contamination of Earth.

\subsection*{Survival and Rescue on LV-426}
\textit{Post-crash survival, June–August 2122} \\
After the Nostromo's destruction, survived on the surface of LV-426 for several weeks with minimal supplies. Documented the environment and the xenomorph's lifecycle. Rescued by a salvage team and provided critical intelligence.

\subsection*{Exposure of Weyland-Yutani's Alien Cover-up}
\textit{Whistleblowing and testimony, 2122–2123} \\
Testified before the Colonial Administration about Weyland-Yutani's knowledge of the xenomorph and their prioritization of the creature over crew safety. Led to increased regulations on deep-space missions and corporate accountability.
